About Crystal Electra

Crystal has taught art and creativity courses at post-secondary institutions and businesses throughout Vancouver, including Capilano College and The University of British Columbia.

She has won several arts awards, including a Senior BC Cultural Fund Award, and a Canada Council Touring Award. She has a Bachelor of Music from the University of British Columbia, where she also studied Fine Art and Architecture.
 
Her work has been widely covered in the media and she's exhibited in Vancouver and beyond.

"My passion is painting, and my gift and joy is to be able to give people the gift of creativity - and discover the artist within all of us." Crystal Electra
