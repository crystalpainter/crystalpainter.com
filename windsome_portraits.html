Winsome Portraits

Portraits in Vancouver, BC, Canada, and the US.

Affordable and inspired! These portrait paintings on canvas are created from photos taken by you, or by the artist. Have a portrait done of your loved one, or pet, and include cherished and meaningful objects. These works of art are focused on portraiture, and will make a wonderful gift for someone special.

These are archival inks & acrylic on canvas and can be created at any size.

Whereas the Home and Garden Portraits weave many elements and sometimes different perspective views into the composition, Winsome Portraits are generally much less expensive, as I can work generally with one photograph. If you want to a few more elements, the price will go up slightly.

Sizes vary.
